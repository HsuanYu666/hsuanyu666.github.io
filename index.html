About Me

I am a staff research engineer with Pixart Taiwan. I received the M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering (Signal Processing) from National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan in 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Chi-Chun (Jeremy) Lee. I am interesting in signal processing, machine learning, big data analysis, and time series analysis.

My recent efforts focus on human-centered behavioral signal processing.
